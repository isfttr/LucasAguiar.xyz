**Last updated June 26, 2026**

This site uses minimal cookies to function properly and to understand how visitors interact with the content.

## Cookies We Use

### PostHog Analytics (privacy-first)

We use [PostHog](https://posthog.com), an open-source analytics platform hosted on our own infrastructure, to collect anonymous usage data. PostHog uses cookies to:

- Track page views and referral sources
- Understand which content is most popular
- Identify usability issues (e.g., broken links, slow pages)

PostHog does **not** share data with third parties. All analytics data stays under our control.

### Google AdSense

This site displays ads via Google AdSense, which may place cookies for interest-based advertising. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

## Your Choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Block all cookies** — the site will still function, but analytics will be limited
- **Use private/incognito mode** — cookies are cleared when you close the browser
- **Opt out of PostHog tracking** — the site respects Do Not Track (DNT) headers
