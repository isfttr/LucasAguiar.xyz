Registering a trademark with INPI is more affordable than many people imagine — especially for micro-entrepreneurs and small businesses, which pay half the value. This guide provides all updated costs based on **Ordinance GM/MDIC nº 110/2025** (in effect since August 2025).

> ⚠️ **Important note:** The values below are from the table in effect in June/2026. INPI updates fees periodically. Always consult the [official costs page](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/custos-e-pagamento) before paying.

---

## Summary of Main Costs

| Service | Full Price | With 50% Discount |
|---------|------------|---------------------|
| **Registration application** (pre-approved specification) | **R$ 880.00** | **R$ 440.00** |
| **Registration application** (free-form specification) | **R$ 1,720.00** | **R$ 860.00** |
| Opposition (per class) | R$ 520.00 | R$ 260.00 |
| Appeal against refusal | R$ 700.00 | R$ 350.00 |
| **First decade + certificate** | **R$ 0.00** 🆓 | **R$ 0.00** 🆓 |
| **Renewal** (10 years) | R$ 1,000.00 | R$ 500.00 |

## 1. Trademark Registration Application

The initial cost depends on how you present the specification of products/services:

**a) Pre-Approved Specification (code 389) — R$ 880.00 (R$ 440.00 with discount)**

You choose products/services from a standardized INPI list (based on the Nice Classification). It is cheaper and reduces the risk of technical requirements. Recommended for most cases.

**b) Free-Form Specification (code 394) — R$ 1,720.00 (R$ 860.00 with discount)**

You write the list of products/services yourself. More expensive and with a higher chance of requirements. Recommended only when the pre-approved specification does not adequately cover what you need.

### Caution: the value is PER CLASS

If your trademark needs to be registered in more than one class (e.g., class 25 for clothing + class 35 for retail services), you pay one application for each class. A trademark in 3 classes costs 3× the value.

---

## 2. First Decade and Certificate — FREE 🆓

**Good news:** since the new 2025 table, the payment of the first decade (code 372) and the issuance of the registration certificate are **automated and free**. You don't need to pay anything extra after the application is approved.

Before the 2025 ordinance, the first decade cost R$ 745.00 — this expense has been eliminated.

---

## 3. Renewal (every 10 years)

Every 10 years, you need to renew the registration:

| Term | Full Price | With Discount |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| Ordinary (within term) | R$ 1,000.00 | R$ 500.00 |
| Extraordinary (with fine) | R$ 2,000.00 | R$ 1,000.00 |

---

## 4. Other Process Costs

### Opposition (code 332) — R$ 520.00 (R$ 260.00)

If someone opposes your trademark application, you need to pay this fee to present your manifestation (response to the opposition).

### Appeal (code 3000) — R$ 700.00 (R$ 350.00)

If INPI refuses your application, you can appeal to the 3rd Vice-Presidency. Each appeal is per class.

### Power of Attorney (code 385) — R$ 90.00

If you appoint an attorney (recommended, especially for companies), the appointment fee is **R$ 90.00** — without discount, as the value is already low.

---

## 5. Who Is Entitled to a 50% Discount?

INPI grants a **50% discount** to:

- ✅ **MEI** (Individual Microentrepreneur)
- ✅ **ME** (Microenterprise)
- ✅ **EPP** (Small Business)
- ✅ Natural persons (without corporate participation in a company in the field)
- ✅ Simple Innovation Companies (LC 167/2019)
- ✅ ICTs (Science and Technology Institutions)
- ✅ Non-profit organizations
- ✅ Public bodies

**Total gratuity (100%)** for individuals registered in **CadÚnico** and people with disabilities.

## 6. Practical Example: How Much Does It Cost to Register "My Brand" in 2 Classes?

Let's simulate the total cost for a **microenterprise** to register a trademark in 2 classes (pre-approved specification), with a power of attorney:

| Item | Full Price | With 50% Discount |
|------|------------|------------------|
| Registration application — Class 1 (code 389) | R$ 880.00 | R$ 440.00 |
| Registration application — Class 2 (code 389) | R$ 880.00 | R$ 440.00 |
| Power of Attorney (code 385) | R$ 90.00 | R$ 90.00 |
| **Total** | **R$ 1,850.00** | **R$ 970.00** |

💡 **Tip:** If you are an MEI or ME, the total cost to register your trademark in **one class** is only **R$ 530.00** (application R$ 440.00 + power of attorney R$ 90.00).

---

## 7. How to Pay

1. Access the [INPI GRU issuance page](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/emissao-de-gru)
2. Select the desired service (e.g., code 389 for trademark application)
3. Fill in your data — if you are entitled to a discount, the system applies it automatically
4. Generate the GRU (Union Collection Guide)
5. Pay at any bank, lottery house, or via internet banking

> 💡 INPI uses an **integrated electronic** system — you generate the GRU, pay, and in a few days the system automatically confirms and the process moves forward.

---

## 8. Cautions and Pitfalls

- **Payment deadline:** the GRU expires in a few days — don't leave it for later
- **Wrong class:** choosing the wrong class is the most common and most expensive error. Consult the [Nice Classification](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/marcas/classificacao-de-nice) before applying
- **Digital power of attorney:** since 2025, the power of attorney can be electronic — you don't need to notarize a signature
- **Undue discount:** if you request the discount without being entitled, INPI may demand supplementation with a fine
