INPI published this Tuesday, July 7, 2026, the public notice for the selection process to grant a research assistant scholarship under the Industrial Property Development Program (PDPI). The project's objective is the **"Consolidated identification of technical divergences between decisions of the 1st and 2nd administrative instances in trademark matters"**.

## Value and Duration

The scholarship has a monthly value of **R$ 6,000.00** and a duration of **12 months**, with possibility of renewal. Activities will be carried out in person at the **General Coordination of Appeals and Administrative Nullity Proceedings (CGREC)** of INPI, in Rio de Janeiro.

## Schedule

| Event | Date |
|--------|------|
| Publication of public notice | 7 de julho de 2026 |
| Start of registration | 8 de julho de 2026 |
| End of registration | 24 de julho de 2026 |

## Requirements

Candidates must meet the following minimum requirements:

- **Bachelor's degree in Law** (mandatory)
- **Postgraduate degree**, preferably in Intellectual Property Law or related area
- Other requirements and supporting documents as set forth in the public notice

## About the Project

The project aims to consolidate and analyze the technical divergences between the decisions of the first and second administrative instances of INPI in trademark matters. Identifying these divergences is an important step towards harmonizing understandings within the Institute, contributing to greater predictability and legal certainty in trademark examinations.

The research will be linked to CGREC, the unit responsible for appeals and administrative nullity proceedings, which indicates that the scholarship holder will have direct contact with INPI's decision-making practice.
