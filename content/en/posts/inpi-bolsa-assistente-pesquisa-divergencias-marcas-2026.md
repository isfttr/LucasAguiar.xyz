INPI published this Tuesday, July 7, 2026, the notice for the selection process to grant a research assistant scholarship under the Industrial Property Development Program (PDPI). The project's objective is the **"Consolidated identification of technical divergences between decisions of the 1st and 2nd administrative instances in trademark matters"**.

## Value and Duration

The scholarship has a monthly value of **R$ 6.000,00** and duration of **12 months**, with the possibility of renewal. Activities will be carried out in person at the **General Coordination of Appeals and Administrative Nullity Processes (CGREC)** of INPI, in Rio de Janeiro.

## Schedule

| Event | Date |
|--------|------|
| Publication of the notice | July 7, 2026 |
| Start of applications | July 8, 2026 |
| Closing of applications | July 24, 2026 |

## Requirements

Candidates must meet the following minimum requirements:

- **Bachelor's degree in Law** (mandatory)
- **Postgraduate degree**, preferably in Intellectual Property Law or related field
- Other requirements and supporting documents specified in the notice

## About the Project

The project aims to consolidate and analyze the technical divergences between the decisions of the first and second administrative instances of INPI in trademark matters. Identifying these divergences is an important step towards harmonizing understandings within the Institute, contributing to greater predictability and legal certainty in trademark examinations.

The research will be linked to CGREC, the unit responsible for appeals and administrative nullity processes, which indicates that the scholarship recipient will have direct contact with INPI's decision-making practice.
