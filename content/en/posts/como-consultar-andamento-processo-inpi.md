Tracking the progress of a process at INPI is essential — whether to know if your trademark has been granted, if your patent has entered examination, or if there is any pending requirement. But the system can be confusing for those who have never used it.

This guide shows you **step by step** how to consult any process at INPI, interpret the progress codes, and not miss important deadlines.

---

## 1. Accessing the e-INPI System

INPI has migrated to the **e-INPI** system (formerly INPI-Sistema). All consultations are online and free.

**Step 1:** Access [https://www.gov.br/inpi](https://www.gov.br/inpi)

**Step 2:** In the main menu, click on **"Services"** and then choose the process type:

- **Trademarks** → "Trademark Search"
- **Patents** → "Patents" → "Patent Search"
- **Industrial Designs** → "Industrial Design Search"
- **Computer Programs** → "Computer Program Search"

**Direct alternative:** Use the [e-INPI Portal](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/perguntas-frequentes-e-inpi) which brings together all services.

---

## 2. Trademark Search

### By process number

1. Go to [Trademark Search](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/marcas/busca)
2. Enter the **process number** (format: `9 digits + 1 verification digit`, e.g.: 938529072)
3. Click "Search"

### By trademark name

1. On the same page, select "Trademark" in the search field
2. Enter the trademark name (e.g.: "LFA CONSULTORIA")
3. Choose the desired class (if known)
4. Click "Search"

### What you will see

The result shows:

| Field | Meaning |
|-------|----------------|
| **Process Number** | Unique application identifier |
| **Trademark** | Trademark name or image |
| **Class** | Nice Classification (products/services) |
| **Owner** | Name of the owner |
| **Status** | Current status (e.g.: "Application published", "Granted", "Denied") |
| **Dispatch** | Last act published in the RPI |

## 3. Patent Search

1. Access [Patent Search](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/patentes/busca)
2. You can search by:
   - **Application number** (PI + year + 7 digits, e.g.: PI2201202200056)
   - **PCT number** (if it's an international filing)
   - **Applicant** (name of the applicant)
   - **Inventor**
   - **Title** (keywords)

### Tip: Advanced search

Use the "Advanced Search" field to combine criteria. Example: applicant = "UNIVERSITY" AND year = "2025" returns all university applications in 2025.

## 4. Understanding Progress Codes (Dispatches)

Each process movement generates a **dispatch** published in the **RPI (Industrial Property Gazette)**. The most common codes:

### Trademarks

| Code | Meaning | What to do |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| **2.1** | Application published | ✅ Process ongoing. Await examination. |
| **3.1** | Formal examination concluded | ✅ Documentation OK. |
| **6.1** | Opposition | ⚠️ Someone has opposed your registration. You have 60 days to contest. |
| **6.4** | Response submitted | ✅ You responded to the opposition. |
| **7.1** | Granted | 🎉 Congratulations! Your trademark has been approved. |
| **7.5** | Denied | ❌ Trademark rejected. You can appeal within 60 days. |
| **8.1** | Appeal filed | ✅ Appeal submitted and under analysis. |
| **8.5** | Appeal granted (deferred) | 🎉 Appeal accepted — trademark registered! |
| **9.1** | Registration granted | ✅ Certificate issued. |

### Patents

| Code | Meaning |
|--------|------------|
| **2.1** | Application published |
| **3.1** | Application publication (18 months after filing) |
| **5.1** | Request for examination |
| **6.1** | Technical examination initiated |
| **7.1** | Technical requirement |
| **9.1** | Decision: grant or refusal |
| **10.1** | Appeal |
| **11.1** | Issuance of patent letter |

## 5. How to Track Without Knowing the Process Number

If you don't have the process number, there are other ways:

### By the owner's CPF/CNPJ

In the trademark search, select "Owner" and enter your CPF or CNPJ. This lists **all processes** linked to that owner.

### By the RPI (Industrial Property Gazette)

The RPI is published **every Tuesday and Thursday** on the INPI website. You can:

1. Access the [RPI page](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/revistas)
2. Choose the section (Trademarks, Patents, Designs)
3. Search by CPF/CNPJ or process number

> 💡 **Pro tip:** Instead of manually reading the RPI, set up process inquiry alerts in the e-INPI system to receive email notifications when there are updates.

---

## 6. Search by CPF/CNPJ (step by step)

This is the most practical way to see **all your processes** at once:

1. Access the [Trademark Search](https://www.gov.br/inpi/pt-br/servicos/marcas/busca)
2. In the "Search by" field, select **"Owner"**
3. Enter your CPF (11 digits) or CNPJ (14 digits) — numbers only
4. Click "Search"
5. The system lists **all trademarks** registered or in process linked to that owner

You can **export the list to CSV** by clicking the download icon — useful for reports or monitoring.

## 7. Useful Apps and Tools

### INPI Mobile
INPI has an app for **Android and iOS** that allows you to consult processes via your phone:
- ✅ Quick search by number
- ✅ Push notifications for new dispatches
- ✅ Access to the RPI

### Automatic alerts
Some private services offer INPI process monitoring with email alerts. The e-INPI system itself has a notification feature — it's worth setting up.

## 8. Important Deadlines

| Event | Deadline | Consequence if Missed |
|--------|-------|------------------------|
| Contest opposition (trademarks) | 60 days from publication | Registration may be archived |
| Appeal against refusal (trademarks) | 60 days | Decision becomes final |
| Appeal against refusal (patents) | 60 days | Loss of right to appeal |
| Annuity payment (patents) | Up to 6 months after due date | Patent may be extinguished |
| Trademark renewal | Up to 6 months after due date | Trademark may be extinguished |
| Response to technical requirement | 90 days (extendable) | Application may be archived |

---

## 9. Common Problems and Solutions

| Problem | Probable Cause | Solution |
|----------|---------------|---------|
| "Process not found" | Incorrect number entered | Check the format (9 digits + verifier for trademarks) |
| "System unavailable" | Scheduled maintenance | Try after 2 hours or the next day |
| Dispatch not appearing | RPI just published | Wait 24-48h for indexing in the search system |
| Outdated data | Physical process (pre-2020) | Consult directly via the RPI |
